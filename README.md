# An Islamic Lyrics analyst
An Islamic Lyrics analyst that gives you an acceptance score for a song after analysing the lyrics quantitatively. Uses Gemini Flash to perform the analysis
1.  **Violation of Tawheed:** Promoting *Shirk* (polytheism), opposing monotheism, or elevating creation to the status of the Creator.
2.  **Promotion of Oppression (Zulm):** Advocating violence, monetary oppression (fraud/conning), or transgressing honor.
3.  **Distortion of Revelation:** Attributing lies to the Divine or fabricating the essence of the Deen.
4.  **Promotion of Indecency (Fahisha):** Glorifying fornication, adultery (*Zina*), or sexual lewdness.
5.  **Violation of Rights (Huquq):** Encouraging the violation of human rights (*Huquq-ul-Ibad*) or cruelty to animals.
These are the five categories it takes into consideration
